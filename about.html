About

Hello, I am Etol, a live coder based in Venice, Italy.
I discovered the practice of live coding in 2015 when I saw a performance by Andrew Sorensen and, through his advice,
I downloaded Sonic Pi and started my journey of learning and experimenting with code and music.
I perform regularly at gigs and streaming events and I am part of a live coding duo named Ctrl-LaShTal
alongside Emme, also a Live Coder, from my hometown.
In February 2020 I have performed alongside Sam Aron @Algorave Prato.
My style has been described as "minimalistic", which is something I am very proud of :)
My first live coding album Shell Shock has been released on Bandcamp on the 12th of November 2021. It consist of 7 live coding improvisations recorded on a train between Venice and Padua in 2021, with no post-editing performed.
