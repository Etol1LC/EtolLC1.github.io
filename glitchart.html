Data Moshing

All of the following are images produced with a technique called Data Moshing or Data Bending which is is the process of manipulating the data of media files in order to achieve visual or auditory effects.
This is known as Glitch Art. All of the following images have been produced only with command line applications such as Vim, Sox, Graphics Magic and some Bash Scripting to automate repetitive tasks.
I am firmly convinced that art belongs to the people, for this reason all of my works are available to everyone for free.
If you are willing to donate you can contact me via email. Thanks!
